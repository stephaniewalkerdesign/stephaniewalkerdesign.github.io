Stephanie Walker Design

Stephanie Walker is the heart and soul of Stephanie Walker Design. Stephanie provides high quality designs whether it be print, graphic, or website design and development. She has excessive knowledge and experience in her field, making her a recognized designer in the Treasure Valley area. Stephanie Walker has worked with companies such as Alletch Appliance LLC, Bitsmart Corporation, and Velma.com.

Stephanie Walker has received many awards for her work in the design field such as the 2013 NCWIT (National Center for Women & Infomation Technology) Idaho Affiliate Award for Aspirations in Computing, SkillsUSA State Champion in Entrepreneurship, and second in the state in Website Design.
